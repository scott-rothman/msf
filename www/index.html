#DROPTHEPRICE

We’re calling on GlaxoSmithKline and Pfizer to drop the price of the lifesaving pneumonia vaccine to $5 per child (for all three doses) in all developing countries.

Most people don’t know that through their (401K) retirement savings plan or through direct investment in mutual funds that they likely have money and influence in these Big Pharmaceutical companies. (Read More)

It is an under-reported fact that pneumococcal disease kills more children under five than any other disease. Nearly one million children die from it each year. [1]

For the past two years, Doctors Without Borders has been campaigning, gathering the support of hundreds of thousands of people from around the world to encourage Pfizer and GSK to reduce the price of the vaccine. Thanks to public pressure, at the end of last year Pfizer and GSK finally announced that they would reduce the price of the vaccine for humanitarian organisations like us. We now need your help to lower the price of the vaccine in all developing countries. We know we can be heard, and the fight is not over.
